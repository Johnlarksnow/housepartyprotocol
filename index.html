FAQ

Why did I receive pUSDC?

Your wallet has transacted with a House Party Protocol contract and/or contracts of House Party Protocol’s partners within in the last 8 weeks. Our system has distributed pUSDC randomly to active users who have transacted with House Party Protocol & partner contracts.


I can not connect to House Party Protocol, is my wallet compatible?

If your wallet does not connect to the website, you might be using an unsupported wallet. Please import your wallet into one of the following providers: MetaMask (Chrome Extension: MetaMask) TrustWallet (Chrome Extension: TrustWallet)


After converting pUSDC tokens into USDC, can I sell the rewarded USDC instantly?

Only 50% of your received pUSDC are unlocked instantly. You can convert those tokens into USDC without any issues. The remaining 50% are transfer-locked and will unlock 30 days after receiving pUSDC.


I am having issues, how can I get help?

Please e-mail rewards@houseparty.exchange for any inquiries regarding hUSDC token distribution. Specify your wallet address as the e-mail title.
